FAQs

1. How are my donations used?

Your donations are directed towards our core focus areas: humanitarian assistance, healthcare, education, development projects, and poverty alleviation.

2. Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We offer several remote volunteering opportunities, including fundraising campaigns, social media advocacy, and administrative support.

3. How do I sponsor a child's education?

Sponsoring a child's education is one of the most impactful ways to help. Please visit our Sponsorship section for more details.

4. How can I partner with AfricaCareLink?

We always seek partnerships with organizations, NGOs, and churches that share our mission. Contact us through our Partnerships page.
